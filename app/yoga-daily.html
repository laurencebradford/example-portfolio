Yoga Daily

A custom-built WordPress blog, featuring daily yoga tips curated from around the web.

Visit the website

Quick Facts

  1. Yoga is something I do daily. So, I wanted to create a blog of curated daily tips.
  2. Since the site has gone live, I have seen a 25% increase in traffic month over month.
  3. Despite it being a passion project, building the blog from the bottom up taught me about creating custom post types in WordPress, troubleshooting cross-browser compatibility issues, and more.

The task

Yoga Daily is a Wordpress blog I created to help others find peace and relieve stress through my favorite kind of exercise. There’s nothing better than my morning yoga session to get me relaxed and “in the zone” before I begin my workday!

What I did

In order to build the Yoga Daily Blog from the ground up, here's what I did:

  1. Brainstorming - What do I want to achieve with my site? How will I accomplish this through my development/design choices?
  2. Content architecture - How will the content exist on the site?
  3. Mockups - Loosely getting an idea of the key components of the site.
  4. Development - Building out the custom WordPress theme, using the Bootstrap framework. Also adding SEO-friendly markup to the code.
  5. Production - Getting the Yoga Daily blog live on the internet so the world could see.

Outcome

Since I first began the site in April 2015, I have seen a 25% increase in page traffic month over month. While the blog may be a passion project, building it from the bottom taught me a lot.

Including:

  1. Custom post types - I created custom post types so I could add my favorite yoga-related products to the site, so readers could purchase them.
  2. Troubleshooting cross-browser compatibility - A few of my design choices did not play nicely across some browsers. This gave me the chance to learn more about making a site look seamless across all web browsers.
  3. Git and Github - This was the first opportunity I had to use Git and Github while creating a project of my own.
