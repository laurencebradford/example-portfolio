The task

TBPR is a wonderful, small-sized firm that is great at what they do. However, their website needed a makeover -- badly. Not only was the site not responsive, it also did not have Schema markup, open graph data, and other important SEO components.

What I did

In order to refresh the site, I took on a __ step approach.

  1. Website and content analysis - the front-end, backend, and site architecture
  2. As I redesigned the WordPress theme, I made it responsive. But also added in important Schema markup to make the site SEO optimized
  3. Later added in appropriate meta titles and descriptions
  4. Got the site hosted with a better provider, VPS, makes site go faster. Set up static assets on a CDN to help with speed.
